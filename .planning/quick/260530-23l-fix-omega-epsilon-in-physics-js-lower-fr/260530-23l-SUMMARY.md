## Summary

Lowered `OMEGA_EPSILON` in `src/physics.js` from `0.5` to `0.05` m/s.

## Root Cause

The free-rolling clamp condition `vehicleSpd + wheelSurfaceSpd < OMEGA_EPSILON` was firing at all speeds below 0.5 m/s combined. At normal low-speed driving (car: ~0.25 m/s, wheels: ~0.13 m/s surface speed), the clamp engaged every tick and forced `wheelOmega = vLong/r` (free-rolling), completely discarding drive torque. The car could not accelerate.

## Fix

`const OMEGA_EPSILON = 0.05` — clamp now only fires below 5 cm/s combined speed, where Euler oscillation is actually a risk. SLIP_EPSILON (0.1 m/s) handles the 0/0 denominator at true standstill.
