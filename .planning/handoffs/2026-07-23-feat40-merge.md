## What the last three commits changed (on top of the 07-22 handoff — read that + memory `project_feat40_tunnels.md` first)

**Params** (data/ranger.js): `tunnelBoreRadius` 8 (was 6.5), `tunnelMinLen` 26 (was 15),
`tunnelMaxLen` 200 (was 700). Sliders recentered (4–12 / 6–46 / 50–350). Fallback `??` values
synced everywhere. Bore density is noticeably LOWER at these defaults — deliberate (user
request); the sliders are the lever if it feels sparse.

**Tube apron** (road-mesh.js `buildTunnelTube`): the below-springing skirt is now a DIRT APRON
riding the exact `_carveDirtY` fold (crown + camber − clearance, 5 cm drop, brown vertex
tint) from under the ribbon edge to the wall base at ±R. The terrain mesh keeps the raw hill
over a bore, so this is the only floor between ribbon edge and tube wall inside the tunnel.
Portal collar widened: `ROUT = R + 2.6`.

**Carve/physics — three connected mechanisms, all shared by mesh AND physics (mesh == collision
is preserved by construction; all in road.js unless noted):**
1. `DEEP_BANK_TOE_EXTRA = 18` (road-carve.js export): deep cuts/fills keep their design slope
   up to ~28 m depth instead of compressing into a vertical face at the old 18 m toe cap.
   Synced across `_carveCrossSection` (cap), `_resolveRoadSurface` interior footprint (now
   36 m; the BUG-21 offEnd radial gate deliberately stays at the base 18 m — widening it
   resurrects the "run ending 40 m away" teleport artifact), and terrain.js `_buildCarveTable`
   `maxExt`.
2. **Rival blend** (`_resolveRoadSurface` → `rival` field; `_carveCrossSectionBlended`): the
   accordion-pleat walls were ownership-flip CLIFFS — a run passing itself (switchback wrapping
   the tunnel spur, found via `_projectOntoRun` with an arc avoid-window ≥ `RIVAL_ARC_SEP` 40 m)
   or a sibling edge, teleporting the carve up to 25 m at the equidistant line. The resolver
   reports the nearest distinct competing pass; the cross-section cross-fades to it over
   `CROSS_BLEND_BAND` 12 m, OFF-RIBBON ONLY (1.5 m fade past the ribbon edge — the deck is
   never blended). GOTCHA: rival candidates are gated on true RADIAL distance and clamped
   projections are rejected (`d2 − signedLat² < 4`) — the avoid window cuts the polyline
   mid-approach and a clamped vertex fakes a nearby deep pass (was a 17 m one-cell notch).
3. **Bore notch** (`_boreNotchCS`, `BORE_NOTCH_SLOPE` 1.2): over a bore span, Y-less/above-apex
   probes get the cut cross-section with its floor rising 1.2 V:H per metre into the span,
   capped at raw → a C0 graded funnel into the arch. This REPLACED both the mesh's 4 m carve
   inset and the portal-line stop face. A "portal neck" (tapering the toe to tube width at
   mouths) was TRIED AND REVERTED — it made the walls steeper (do not retry).

**Terrain shader** (terrain.js, mouth cutout): skin discards only ABOVE the apron plane
(`tq.y > -0.7`) and only from a FLAT plane 1.2 m outside the portal inward (`ta < -1.2 →
continue`) — the capsule end-cap sphere used to bulge ~R past the mouth and eat the ground
disc outside (user captures 1784820350735 / the Image-12/13 gaps). Shader is baked into the
terrain material at boot — page RELOAD required after edits, HMR won't apply it.

**Gate** (test/shoulder-lateral-continuity.mjs): new `BANK_TOL = 0.5` tier where the pinned
resolve's rival is in blend range or the station is within 40 m + LAT_MAX of a bore span —
deliberate graded banks, C0, steps scale with DLAT. Judged from the PINNED nr0 (what the sweep
actually samples), not a fresh resolve.
