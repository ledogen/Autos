<objective>
P1 — the single road-query API. Make ONE surface the whole codebase goes through
so new features can't re-fragment the road. Two forms:
- `byArc(runKey, arcS) → RoadSample` for consumers that already have arcS
  (ribbon, carve, physics).
- `sampleRoadAt(x, z) → RoadSample | null` for world queries — `queryNearest`
  becomes the PROJECTOR (it keeps the `_tiles` block acceleration + 09-17
  projection refine to FIND `(runKey, arcS)`); everything geometric is then read
  from the P0 `runProfile` by `arcS`, so it is seam-continuous.

Implement-now-minimal: only the fields BUG-14/12/10 need
(`gradeY`, `tangent`, `camber`, `crown`, `blendW`, `arcS`, `runKey`). Carry
`surfaceType`/`onRoad` in the struct and route the obvious callers through the
API, but do NOT build friction/effects/props now — leave the seam so they slot in
later without another refactor.

Purpose: stop the road being re-fragmented by every consumer.
Output: RoadSample + byArc + sampleRoadAt in src/road.js.
</objective>
