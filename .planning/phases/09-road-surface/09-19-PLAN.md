<objective>
Introduce a single generation counter on RoadSystem as the ONE invalidation source (D1). The counter bumps on every re-stream AND re-route (invalidateCache, debouncedRoadRebuild). Ribbon tiles (road-mesh.js) and terrain-carve chunks (terrain.js) record the generation they were built against; on each ring sync, any tile/chunk whose stored generation ≠ current is rebuilt. This fixes bug #1 (stale ribbon diverging from spline → car falls through) and bug #6 (maxGrade slider re-routes but doesn't rebuild the carve) with one mechanism.

Purpose: Everything downstream (D2 camber cache, design-grade caches) hangs off this versioning. It must land before D2/D3.
Output: `_generation` + `roadGeneration()` on RoadSystem; `builtGeneration` on ribbon tiles; `builtRoadGeneration` on carve chunks; version-mismatch rebuild wiring in both ring-sync paths.
</objective>
